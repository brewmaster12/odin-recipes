Spagbol

Description

Spahgetti and bolognese, a.k.a spagbol. Good when you don't want meatballs.

Ingedients

  • 166g Spahgetti
  • 400g mince meat
  • Dolmino bolognese

Steps

  1. Boil pot of water
  2. Put 166g spahgetti in water when boiled. Boil for 10 mins
  3. While waiting for water to boil, start to cook the mince
  4. When mince is brown add the bolognese
  5. Cook for 10 mins
